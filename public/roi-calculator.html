Executive Briefing

Mental Fitness for Healthcare Cost Reduction

The root cause of high-cost medical events is often the same: unaddressed, sub-clinical mental distress. An employee struggling with chronic stress is a "pre-patient." If left unmanaged, this distress predictably escalates into ER visits, hospital admissions, and prolonged claims.

The Clinical Precedent

MGH: 43% reduction in total healthcare utilization. Kaiser Permanente: 50% ED reduction, 80% drop in admissions. AHA: heart disease patients were 75% less likely to be rehospitalized with mental health treatment.

The Business Case

For every $1 invested, medical costs fall by $3.27 and absenteeism costs fall by $2.73 (Baicker et al., Harvard, 2010).

Mental distress is a pre-condition for high-cost physical health events. SkillfulMeans applies evidence-based mental fitness protocols — validated in hospital systems — to the corporate employee population, intercepting costly events before they hit the claims ledger.
